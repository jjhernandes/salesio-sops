Section 1
Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is for Coach to provide sales coaching, training, consulting, and performance improvement services to Client and its sales team during the term of this Agreement.

Section 2
Term

The coaching engagement shall commence on October 1, 2026, and shall continue for a period of twelve (12) consecutive weeks, ending on December 24, 2026, unless terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

Section 3
Services

During the twelve-week engagement, Coach shall provide:

Closer Coaching
Three (3) closer coaching calls per week.
Setter Coaching
Two (2) setter coaching calls per week.
Additional Support
Coach may provide implementation guidance, sales process recommendations, call reviews, objection handling assistance, sales strategy consulting, and operational support as reasonably necessary throughout the engagement.

Scheduling of calls shall be mutually agreed upon by the Parties.

Payment Terms

Payments shall be made through Stripe, wire transfer, or another payment method approved by Coach.

If any payment remains unpaid for more than five (5) days after its due date, Coach may suspend services until payment is received in full.

Client shall remain responsible for all amounts due under this Agreement.

Section 6
No Refunds

Client acknowledges that coaching services, consulting, intellectual property, and access to Coach's expertise begin immediately upon engagement.

All payments made under this Agreement are final and non-refundable. No refunds, chargebacks, reversals, credits, or cancellations shall be permitted for services rendered, scheduled, or made available under this Agreement.
Section 7
Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

  • Attend scheduled coaching sessions.
  • Ensure relevant team members attend coaching sessions.
  • Implement recommendations in a timely manner.
  • Provide reasonable access to information necessary for coaching and consulting purposes.
  • Actively participate in the coaching process.

Client acknowledges that results are dependent upon implementation and execution.

No Guarantee of Results

Coach does not guarantee any specific revenue increase, sales performance improvement, hiring outcome, appointment volume, conversion rate, profitability, or business result.

Client understands that coaching services provide guidance, education, and strategic recommendations only. Business results depend on numerous factors outside Coach's control, including execution, market conditions, product quality, sales personnel performance, and economic conditions.
