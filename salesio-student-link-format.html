How It Works

?name=
Shows their name in the greeting, the "Viewing as" badge, and on the Graduation certificate. Saved to their browser after the first visit — they don't need the link again after that.
&start=
Their enrollment date, format YYYY-MM-DD. Used to calculate their current week (days since start ÷ 7). Graduation and Renewal Options stay locked with a live countdown until this crosses Week 10.

3

When to Send

Day One
Send the Student HQ link alongside the Day One Expectations doc. This is their home base for the full 12 weeks.
If they ever lose it
Rebuild the same link from their Enrollment Agreement (name + Cohort Start Date) and resend. It's safe to send the same link twice — nothing breaks by re-sending.
4

Common Mistakes

⚠️
Don't hand-type the URLSpaces in names have to be encoded correctly or the link breaks. Always use the Link Builder above instead of typing the URL by hand.
⚠️
Wrong start date = wrong lock stateIf Graduation unlocks too early or too late for a student, the start date on their link doesn't match their actual Cohort Start Date. Rebuild and resend.
⚠️
Not real securityThis is a UX nudge, not access control — these are public pages with no login. Don't rely on it to actually restrict who can see a page, only to shape the normal path through the program.
