Cancellation & Refunds

Before your cohort starts
Cancellation requests made before your Cohort Start Date are eligible for a full refund, minus any payment processing fees already incurred.
After your cohort starts
Once your cohort begins, the program fee is non-refundable as a general policy. At Sales.io's sole discretion, an exception may be considered on a documented, case-by-case basis, but only for students who met every threshold in Section 3: Participation Requirements throughout their enrollment.
⚖️
If thresholds aren't metStudents who did not meet the Participation Requirements in Section 3 are not eligible for any refund, credit, or adjustment. Any dispute filed on this basis will be contested using Sales.io's attendance and activity records.

Dispute Resolution & Legal Terms

Binding arbitration
Any dispute arising from this Agreement will be resolved through individual, binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association, rather than in court — except claims that qualify for small claims court, which either party may still bring there.
Class action waiver
All claims must be brought individually. Neither party may bring a claim as part of a class, collective, or representative action, or consolidate a claim with any other student's.
Limitation of liability
Sales.io's total liability under this Agreement, for any reason, is capped at the amount you paid for the program. Sales.io is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, including lost income or lost business opportunity.
Entire agreement
This document is the entire agreement between you and Sales.io. Statements made on sales calls, in marketing materials, testimonials, or anywhere else that are not written in this Agreement do not modify it.
Notice before disputing
Before filing a dispute, chargeback, or claim of any kind, you agree to first notify Sales.io in writing and allow 15 business days to respond and attempt resolution directly.
Governing law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida, without regard to conflict of law principles.
Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is found unenforceable, every remaining provision stays in full effect.
