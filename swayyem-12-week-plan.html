12-Week Clarity Plan

A step-by-step roadmap to go from Matthew closing everything at sub-10% to a system that closes 25-30% without him on every call.

3 Phases
12 Weeks
25-30% Target Close Rate
+$250k Monthly Revenue Goal
Phase 1 Build the Foundation Weeks 1 – 4
W1
Get the House in Order
  • Audit current iClosed setup — what exists, what is missing
  • Map the sales process from lead in to contract signed
  • Identify the 3 biggest drop-off points in the pipeline
  • Pull 5 recorded calls and review together — this is where the framework gets grounded in reality
W2
Build the Communication Infrastructure
  • Build pre-call sequence in iClosed (confirmation, 24hr reminder, same-day text)
  • Build post-call 1 sequence with proper wait steps
  • Build post-call 2 sequence
  • Test all sequences end to end before going live
W3
Finalize the Sales Framework
  • Refine the SIGNAL framework based on Week 1 call recordings
  • Build the objection handling bank from real objections Matthew has heard
  • Document the setter-to-closer briefing standard
  • Define the offer decision tree: when to use each entry offer
W4
KPI Infrastructure
  • Set up HubSpot pipeline stages to match the actual sales process
  • Define the weekly reporting standard for reps
  • Establish baseline numbers: close rate, show rate, avg deal size
  • Everything to measure progress is in place before new reps touch anything
Phase 2 Bring in the Team Weeks 5 – 8
W5
Hiring
  • Run closer candidates through the interview SOP and scorecard
  • Roleplay screen before any offer goes out
  • Target: hire one closer by end of week
W6
Onboarding
  • New closer reads the handbook on Day 1
  • Shadows 3 live calls with Matthew
  • Reviews 3 recorded calls independently with notes
  • Roleplays SIGNAL framework with feedback before going live
W7
First Live Calls
  • New closer runs calls with Matthew in the room
  • Debrief after every call: what worked, what to adjust
  • Matthew is coaching, not saving the deal
  • Weekly KPI check-in begins
W8
First Independent Calls
  • Closer runs calls solo, recordings reviewed by Matthew
  • Track show rate, close rate, deal size from day one
  • Identify the 2-3 adjustments needed to move from good to great
Phase 3 Optimize and Scale Weeks 9 – 12
W9
Offer Testing
  • Review conversion data from each entry offer
  • Test one offer variation based on what the data shows
  • Review outbound sequences and adjust messaging based on response rates
W10
Process Refinement
  • Pull the 10 most recent closed deals and 10 most recent losses
  • Find the pattern: what wins have in common, where losses break down
  • Update the SIGNAL framework and objection bank with real data
W11
Scale What Works
  • If closer is hitting 20%+: decide whether to hire a second rep or optimize further
  • Review iClosed sequences: open rates, reply rates, drop-off points
  • Matthew should be off the floor for most calls by this point
W12
Full Review
  • Compare Week 12 numbers to baseline from Week 4
  • Close rate, show rate, deal size, monthly revenue
  • Identify the one biggest lever left to pull going into Q3
  • Present a clear picture: here is where you were, here is where you are, here is what is next
