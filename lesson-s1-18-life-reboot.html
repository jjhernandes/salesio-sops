Life Reboot Protocol

Print this. Complete it by hand. This is where the identity work stops being intellectual and becomes real. You cannot do this in your head.

You've spent 17 lessons building a framework for understanding who you are and why you show up the way you do on calls. This is where it lands. Print this page. Sit somewhere quiet. Answer every section honestly, not the way you want the answer to be. The version of you who's honest in this exercise is closer to the rep you want to be than the version who writes impressive answers.

Part 03 The New Operating System

Describe the rep you need to become. Write in third person — this creates useful distance from your current self-image and lets you describe someone you're building toward, not defending.

Who is the rep who closes at the level you want to close at?
Describe them in 3rd person. What do they believe about sales? About prospects? About themselves? How do they show up when things are hard? What do they not need from the people they talk to?
Write your answer here. (e.g., "This rep walks into every call from a place of...")
What does that rep do on call days?
Specific behaviors. Morning to evening. What their pre-call routine looks like, how they review calls, how they handle a loss.
Write your answer here.
What does that rep do off call days?
What inputs are they protecting? What are they building between call days that shows up as their state on call days?
Write your answer here.
What does that rep do when they lose a deal?
Specifically. Not "they learn from it" — what do they actually do in the hour after a hard loss?
Write your answer here.
