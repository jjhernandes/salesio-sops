Section 01
Relationship of the Parties

Contractor is engaged as an independent contractor and not as an employee, partner, agent, or joint venturer of the Company. Nothing in this agreement shall be interpreted or construed as creating an employer-employee relationship.

Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, including self-employment taxes. The Company will not withhold federal, state, or local income taxes from any compensation paid to Contractor. Contractor will receive a Form 1099 at year-end if applicable under IRS thresholds.

Contractor retains the right to perform services for other clients and businesses, provided such work does not create a conflict of interest or violate any term of this agreement.

Section 02
Scope of Services

Contractor agrees to perform appointment setting services on behalf of the Company, including but not limited to:

  • Identifying, contacting, and qualifying prospective students via direct message, phone, or other agreed-upon channels
  • Scheduling sales calls between qualified prospects and the Company's closing team
  • Conducting triage calls to pre-qualify leads before close calls when directed
  • Logging all lead activity, stage updates, and call notes in Close CRM accurately and in real time
  • Following up with prospects across the pipeline to maintain momentum and reduce no-show rates
  • Communicating pipeline updates and issues to the Company's team via Slack in a timely manner

Contractor shall not make binding commitments, financial guarantees, or program representations on behalf of the Company beyond what is explicitly authorized.

Section 03
Compensation

Contractor shall be compensated solely on a performance basis as follows:

Activity Rate Conditions
Closed Deal Commission
Days 1–60 (Probationary)		 3% of deal value Paid on deals where Contractor set the original appointment that resulted in a close. Deal value is the total collected amount ($7,500 PIF or $10,000 on 3-way split). Applies for the first 60 days from the Effective Date.
Closed Deal Commission
Day 61+ (Standard)		 5% of deal value Rate increases to 5% automatically on day 61, provided Contractor remains in good standing. No action required by either party.
Completed Triage Call
Day 61+ only		 $50 flat Paid per completed triage call where Contractor ran the qualification conversation and the prospect showed up. No-shows and cancellations do not qualify. This rate becomes available after the 60-day probationary period.
There is no base pay, retainer, or guaranteed minimum. Compensation is 100% performance-based. The 60-day probationary period is designed to ensure mutual fit before full rates apply.
  • Payment schedule: Commissions are paid on a bi-weekly basis, on the 1st and 15th of each month.
  • Clawback: If a deal is refunded or charged back within 90 days, the corresponding commission will be reversed or deducted from the next pay period.
  • Attribution: Contractor receives commission credit only for deals where they set the original qualifying appointment and where that appointment is documented in Close CRM prior to the call. Undocumented sets are not eligible for commission.
  • Split pays: Commission on installment deals is calculated on the full deal value and paid after the first payment clears, provided the deal remains active and not in dispute.
  • Disputes: Any commission dispute must be raised in writing within 14 days of the pay date in question.
Section 04
Confidentiality

During and after the term of this agreement, Contractor agrees to keep confidential all non-public information belonging to or shared by the Company, including but not limited to:

  • Lead lists, prospect data, and CRM records
  • Student names, contact information, and enrollment details
  • Pricing, offer structure, and commission rates
  • Internal scripts, qualification processes, and setting frameworks
  • Revenue figures, team structure, and operational data

Contractor shall not share, sell, or disclose any of the above to any third party without prior written consent from the Company.

Section 05
Non-Solicitation

During the term of this agreement and for a period of 12 months after termination, Contractor agrees not to:

  • Directly solicit, recruit, or approach any current or former Sales.io student for the purpose of selling them a competing sales training program or service
  • Use any lead data, contact lists, or CRM records accessed during the term of this agreement to contact prospects on behalf of a competitor
  • Solicit or recruit any current Sales.io team member, coach, or contractor to leave the Company

This clause does not restrict Contractor from general employment or from working with other companies in the sales training space, provided they do not use Company data or directly target Company students and leads.

Section 06
Ownership of Work and Data

All lead data, CRM records, prospect notes, and materials created by Contractor in connection with services performed under this agreement are and shall remain the sole property of the Company. Contractor shall have no claim to any such data upon termination.

Upon termination, Contractor agrees to immediately cease access to all Company systems including Close CRM, Slack, and any other platforms, and to not retain copies of any Company data.

Section 07
Performance Standards

Contractor acknowledges and agrees to the following minimum performance expectations:

  • Maintain a booked appointment show rate of no less than 60% on a rolling 30-day basis
  • Log all lead interactions in Close CRM within the same business day they occur
  • Respond to inbound leads within 5 minutes during agreed working hours
  • Communicate all no-shows, cancellations, and pipeline issues to the team via Slack promptly

Failure to maintain these standards may result in a performance review and, at the Company's discretion, termination of this agreement.

Section 08
Term and Termination

This agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues on an at-will basis until terminated by either party.

  • Termination by either party: Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with 7 days written notice.
  • Immediate termination by Company: The Company may terminate this agreement immediately and without notice for: breach of confidentiality, solicitation of students or team members, falsification of CRM records, gross negligence, or any conduct that materially harms the Company or its students.
  • Final payment: Upon termination, Contractor will be paid all earned and undisputed commissions for closed deals and completed triages through the termination date, minus any applicable clawbacks.
Section 09
Representations and Warranties

Contractor represents and warrants that:

  • They have the legal right to enter into this agreement and that doing so does not violate any other agreement they are party to
  • They will perform services in a professional manner consistent with industry standards
  • They will not make false, misleading, or unauthorized representations to any prospect on behalf of the Company
  • They will immediately notify the Company of any conflict of interest that arises during the term of this agreement
Section 10
Governing Law and Disputes

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida, without regard to conflict of law principles.

Any dispute arising under this agreement that cannot be resolved informally between the parties shall be submitted to binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney's fees and costs.

Section 11
Entire Agreement

This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter herein and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, or agreements, whether oral or written.

This agreement may be modified only by a written amendment signed by both parties. Waiver of any provision on one occasion shall not constitute a waiver on any other occasion.
