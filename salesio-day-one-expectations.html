Your weekly 1-on-1

Private coaching — every week, no exceptions

Your 1-on-1 is recurring — same time, every week for 12 weeks. Each session is built around the curriculum module for that week. Come having done the module and you'll get ten times more out of the call.

📞
How to use your 1-on-1
Recurring weekly session — tied to that week's curriculum module.
1
Your slot is already booked and recurring. Same time every week. If you need to reschedule, do it inside Circle — don't just no-show.
2
Complete that week's module before you join. The 1-on-1 is where you apply what the module covered — not where you hear it for the first time. Your coach expects you to have done it.
3
Bring your numbers. Close rate, calls per week, average deal size, income this month. If you don't track it, you can't coach it.
4
Submit a call before your 1-on-1. The most productive sessions happen when your coach has already heard you on a call. Give them something to work with.
5
Do the assigned work before the next session. If your coach tells you to do something — do it. Accountability is not optional inside this program.
Call reviews

Submit a call every week

Call reviews are how the coaching team sees exactly where your ceiling is. You cannot improve something you're not watching. Submit every week without exception.

🎥
How to submit your call

Submit your recordings in the #call-review channel inside Circle. One call minimum per week. The reps who submit the most calls improve the fastest, every time.

1
Record your sales call on Zoom, Loom, or your dialer — any platform works
2
Post the link in #call-review on Circle with a short note: what went well, where you felt it slip
3
Your call gets reviewed on Tuesday's Call Review session — live in front of the group
4
Implement the feedback that same week — not next month, not when it feels right

Non-negotiables

The expectations are simple

These are the five things that separate the students who hit $30K months from the ones who don't.

01
Show up to your 1-on-1 every week. If you need to reschedule, reschedule — don't cancel without rebooking. Every skipped session is a week of your 12 weeks gone.
02
Submit a call recording every week. It does not have to be a perfect call. In fact, the messy calls are the most valuable. Submit something.
03
Complete at least one module per week. The curriculum is the foundation everything else is built on. Don't skip it because the group calls feel more urgent.
04
Be active in the community. Post in Circle. Ask questions. Share wins. The people who hide in the background and consume without contributing fall behind.
05
Do the assigned work between sessions. When a coach gives you something to implement — a frame to try, a language pattern, a call structure — do it before the next session.
