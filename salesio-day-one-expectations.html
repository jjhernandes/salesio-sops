Your weekly 1-on-1

Private coaching — every week, no exceptions

Your 1-on-1 is the most direct lever in this program. This is not a check-in. It is a working session. Come prepared or you'll waste it.

📞
How to use your 1-on-1
Every week. Book it, show up, do the work.
1
Book it in advance. Your slot doesn't hold itself. Use the booking link in Skool to schedule the same time each week. Consistency here matters.
2
Come with a real question or a real problem. "Things are going okay" is not a coaching prompt. What's your close rate right now? Where did you lose the last deal? What objection is killing you? Know before you join.
3
Bring your numbers. Close rate, calls per week, average deal size, income this month. If you don't track it, you can't coach it.
4
Submit a call before your 1-on-1. The most productive sessions happen when your coach has already heard you on a call. Give them something to work with.
5
Do the thing that was assigned before the next session. If your coach tells you to do something — do it. Accountability is not optional inside this program.
Call reviews

Submit a call every week

Call reviews are how the coaching team sees exactly where your ceiling is. You cannot improve something you're not watching. Submit every week without exception.

🎥
How to submit your call
Submit your recordings in the #call-review WhatsApp channel. One call minimum per week. More is better — the reps who submit the most calls improve the fastest, every time.
1
Record your sales call on Zoom, Loom, or your dialer — any platform works
2
Post the link in #call-review on WhatsApp with a short note: what went well, where you felt it slip
3
Your call gets reviewed on Tuesday's Call Review session — live in front of the group
4
Implement the feedback that same week — not next month, not when it feels right

Non-negotiables

The expectations are simple

You will not get the result if you treat this like a course you bought and forgot about. These are the five things that separate the students who hit $30K months from the ones who don't.

01
Show up to your 1-on-1 every week. If you need to reschedule, reschedule — don't cancel without rebooking. Every skipped session is a week of your 12 weeks gone.
02
Submit a call recording every week. It does not have to be a perfect call. In fact, the messy calls are the most valuable. Submit something.
03
Complete at least one module per week. The curriculum is the foundation everything else is built on. Don't skip it because the group calls feel more urgent.
04
Be active in the community. Post in WhatsApp. Ask questions. Share wins. The people who hide in the background and consume without contributing fall behind. The community is a tool — use it.
05
Do the assigned work between sessions. When a coach gives you something to implement — a frame to try, a language pattern to practice, a call structure to run — do it before the next session. Coaching only works if you act on it.
