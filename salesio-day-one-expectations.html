04 — Your weekly 1-on-1

Private coaching, every week

Your 1-on-1 is recurring — same time, every week for 12 weeks. What you consumed and experienced in the group calls that week gets brought into the session and worked through with your coach.

1
Your slot is already booked and recurring
Same time every week. If you need to reschedule, do it inside Circle — don't just no-show. Every skipped session is a week of your 12 weeks gone.
2
Show up with what came up that week
What landed from the group calls? What confused you? Where did you lose a deal? What did you try that didn't work? The 1-on-1 is where you process and apply it — not where you hear things for the first time.
3
Bring your numbers
Close rate, calls per week, average deal size, income this month. If you don't track it, you can't coach it.
4
Submit a call before your session
The most productive 1-on-1s happen when your coach has already heard you on a call. Give them something real to work with.
5
Do the assigned work before the next session
When your coach tells you to try something — a frame, a language pattern, a call structure — do it that week. Accountability is not optional in this program.
05 — Call reviews

Submit a call every week

Call reviews are how the coaching team sees exactly where your ceiling is. You cannot improve something you're not watching. One call minimum, every week, without exception.

1
Record your sales call on Zoom, Loom, or your dialer — any platform works
2
Post the link in Daily Execution on Circle with a short note: what went well, where you felt it slip
3
Your call gets reviewed on Tuesday's Call Review session — live in front of the group
4
Implement the feedback that same week — not next month, not when it feels right

07 — Non-negotiables

The five things that actually matter

These separate the students who hit $30K months from the ones who stay stuck.

01
Show up to your 1-on-1 every week. If you need to reschedule, reschedule — don't cancel without rebooking. Every skipped session is a week of your 12 weeks gone.
02
Submit a call recording every week. It does not have to be a perfect call. The messy ones are the most valuable. Submit something.
03
Stay current with the curriculum. The content fuels the group calls and your 1-on-1. Skip it and you lose the context your coaching depends on.
04
Be active in Circle. Post wins. Ask questions. Share what's not working. The people who hide in the background and consume without contributing fall behind every time.
05
Do the assigned work between sessions. When your coach tells you to try something — do it before the next session. Coaching only works when you act on it.
