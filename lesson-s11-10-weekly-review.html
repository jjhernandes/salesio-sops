Weekly Performance Review

Complete this every Sunday or Monday morning. 20 minutes. One focused session where you review the week, find the pattern, and set your intention for the next 5 days.

The rule: Don't do this in your head. Write it down. The act of writing forces specificity, and specificity reveals things you can actually fix.
