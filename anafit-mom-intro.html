Mom Group Chat — Intro Script

What JJ says when adding Ana to the group chat with his mom
Step 1 — JJ adds both to the chat & opens with this
Hey Mom! I wanted to connect you with Ana — she's an incredible coach and I genuinely think working with her is going to be one of the best decisions you make. Ana, this is my mom! 🙌
Short, warm, personal. Sets the tone for both of them.
Step 2 — What Ana says right after
Hi! So happy to be connected with you — JJ has told me so much, and I'm really excited to get to know you. I'm going to take great care of you, I promise 🌸 Can't wait to chat!
Warm and confident. No sales language. Just human connection.
Step 3 — Ana follows up same day to get things moving
Whenever you're ready, I'd love to hop on a quick call so we can get to know each other and I can learn a little about your goals. No pressure at all — just want to make sure we get started on the right foot! 😊
Light, low pressure. Moves to a call without feeling like a pitch.
