How It Works

1

You make the introduction

When you have someone ready to refer, send a warm introduction to the Sales.io team. This can be a DM on Instagram, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn — or a referral through your unique partner link. Warm introductions close at a significantly higher rate than cold link clicks. If you can send a personal message alongside the referral, do it.

2

We take it from there

Once you make the intro, Sales.io handles everything — qualification call, discovery, enrollment. You do not need to sell the program, handle pricing questions, or manage objections. Your job is the introduction. Ours is the close.

3

You get paid

When the referral enrolls and their first payment clears, your commission is triggered. You will receive a confirmation with the enrollment details and the commission amount. You can track every referral and its status in your partner dashboard at any time.

Attribution and Tracking
Referrals tracked via your unique partner link and direct introduction logs
Link click attributed to you if the prospect enrolls within 60 days
Warm introductions attributed to you regardless of timing
Attribution disputes always resolved in favor of the referring partner

Payment Terms

Pay in full — $7,500 Commission paid within 7 days of payment clearing
2-way split — $3,750 × 2 Commission on each payment paid within 7 days of that payment clearing
3-way split — $3,333 × 3 Commission on each payment paid within 7 days of that payment clearing
Klarna / Affirm Commission paid in full within 14 days of financing approval
Payment Method

Bank transfer or PayPal depending on your preference and location. Confirmed when you join the program.

Minimum Payout

$500 per cycle. If your earned commission is below $500, it rolls to the next cycle.

Clawback policy: In the rare event a student invokes the money-back guarantee, commission is clawed back proportionally. In program history, only one refund has ever been issued — for a medical emergency, not dissatisfaction. This is included for transparency, not because it is likely to affect you.

Partner Rules

What we ask of you
Only refer people you genuinely believe are a fit. The program works when the students are serious.
Do not make earnings claims beyond the documented track record. It speaks for itself — use it.
Set honest expectations. This is 12 weeks of real work — not passive income for the student.
If a referral has questions about the program, send them to the team rather than answering on our behalf.
What removes you from the program
Misrepresenting the program, the results, or the pricing
Referring people who are not a genuine fit in order to collect commission
Any behavior that damages the Sales.io brand or community

The program is based on trust. That goes both ways.
