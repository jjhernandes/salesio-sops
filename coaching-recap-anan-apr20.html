Revenue vs. Cash Collected Revenue is what's sold. Cash collected is what's actually paid — after payment plans and financing, it's typically 80-90% of revenue. Your commission comes from cash collected, not total revenue.
15K/month path
Find a role on an offer doing $250K+/month. Generate $150K in cash collected. At 10% = $15K. This is achievable as a strong closer on the right offer.
40K/month path
That requires an offer doing $800K-$1M/month. One closer at that level generated $400K alone — half the company's revenue. That's the ceiling you're eventually building toward.
