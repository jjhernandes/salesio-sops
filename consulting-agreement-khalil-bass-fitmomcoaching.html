Sales System Buildout & Rep Placement Advisory Agreement
This Sales System Buildout & Rep Placement Advisory Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of _______________, 2026 (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

Sales.io, LLC, a Florida limited liability company ("Sales.io"), represented by JJ Hernandez, Founder,

and

FitMom Coaching, LLC ("Client"), represented by Khalil Bass, Founder.

Sales.io and Client may each be referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."
1. Purpose

Client desires to engage Sales.io for advisory and operational support in building and optimizing Client's internal sales function.

Sales.io will work directly with Client to:

  • Source, vet, and place qualified sales representatives on Client's team
  • Build out Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and sales processes
  • Provide access to Sales.io's training curriculum and coaching infrastructure
  • Conduct 1-on-1 coaching sessions with Client and Client's team
  • Design and implement a team cadence and accountability structure
  • Source and place a long-term Sales Manager on Client's team

Sales.io agrees to provide these services under the terms outlined in this Agreement.

2. Scope of Services
2.1 Sales Rep Sourcing & Placement

Sales.io will source, vet, and place qualified sales representatives on the FitMom Coaching team for the duration of the initial 3-month engagement.

Services include:

  • Reviewing candidates from Sales.io's network
  • Conducting initial screening and vetting on behalf of Client
  • Presenting qualified candidates for Client approval
  • Facilitating the onboarding of placed representatives
  • Replacing underperforming reps within the engagement period at Sales.io's discretion

Sales.io does not guarantee that candidates will accept offers or remain employed long-term.

2.2 SOP Development

Sales.io will build a complete set of Standard Operating Procedures tailored to FitMom Coaching's sales process and offer.

Services include:

  • Discovery and qualification call frameworks
  • Pitch and close process documentation
  • Objection handling guides specific to Client's offer
  • Daily and weekly activity standards for the sales team
  • CRM usage and pipeline management protocols

All SOPs and sales process documents created under this Agreement are the sole property of Client upon delivery.

2.3 Curriculum & Training Access

All placed sales representatives and designated FitMom Coaching team members will receive access to the full Sales.io training curriculum for the duration of the Agreement.

This includes:

  • Access to Sales.io's Skool platform and all training modules
  • Identity, psychology, process, and advanced influence curriculum
  • Call recordings, coaching replays, and training materials
2.4 Coaching Call Access

Placed representatives and eligible team members will have live access to all weekly group coaching calls inside Sales.io, including:

  • Mock Call Monday
  • Setting Mastery
  • Coffee Chat
  • Sales Psychology
  • Call Review / Game Tape
  • Inner Circle Advanced
  • B2B Strategy (where applicable)
2.5 1-on-1 Coaching Sessions

Sales.io will conduct regular 1-on-1 coaching sessions with Khalil Bass and designated members of the FitMom Coaching team.

Sessions are focused on:

  • Offer and messaging alignment
  • Team performance review
  • Individual rep development
  • Strategic direction and pipeline growth

Session frequency and format to be agreed upon at the start of the engagement.

2.6 Team Cadence Development

Sales.io will design and implement a team cadence structure for the FitMom Coaching sales team.

This includes:

  • Daily and weekly rhythm for the sales floor
  • Call review and accountability frameworks
  • Performance tracking and KPI structure
  • Communication norms and feedback loops between reps, manager, and Client
2.7 Sales Manager Placement (Long-Term)

Upon successful completion of the initial 3-month engagement, Sales.io will source and place a qualified Sales Manager on the FitMom Coaching team on a long-term basis.

The Sales Manager will be responsible for:

  • Day-to-day oversight of the sales team
  • Rep coaching, call review, and performance management
  • Pipeline management and reporting to Client
  • Maintaining and evolving the SOPs developed during the initial engagement

Sales Manager compensation is to be agreed upon separately between Client and Sales Manager at the time of placement. Sales.io's role is sourcing and vetting only.

4. Sales Rep Placement & Replacement Support
4.1 Candidate Quality Standards

Sales.io agrees to present candidates who meet the following minimum qualification standards unless otherwise agreed in writing:

  • Prior experience in sales, appointment setting, or closing
  • Verifiable work history or relevant sales performance indicators
  • Successful completion of Sales.io's screening process
  • Alignment with the role description provided by Client
4.2 Placement Effort Commitment

Sales.io will continue reasonable sourcing and screening efforts until Client has successfully hired a candidate who meets the agreed role criteria. Sales.io may present multiple candidates and will assist throughout the interview and hiring process. Sales.io does not guarantee that a candidate will accept an offer or remain employed long-term.

4.3 Replacement Guarantee

If a sales representative placed through Sales.io voluntarily resigns or is terminated by Client for performance-related reasons within twelve (12) months of their hire date, Sales.io will resume sourcing efforts and make commercially reasonable efforts to present a qualified replacement within forty-five (45) days of notification.

4.4 Conditions for Replacement Support

Replacement support applies provided that Client:

  • Provided the representative with access to necessary tools and systems
  • Provided a clear compensation structure consistent with the role
  • Explained the product/service and sales process
  • Provided at least two (2) weeks of onboarding support after hire

Replacement support does not apply if the rep leaves due to:

  • Non-payment or delayed payment by Client
  • Significant change to compensation structure or job scope
  • Hostile work environment or illegal conduct
5. Candidate Ownership

Any candidate introduced by Sales.io shall be considered a Sales.io candidate for a period of twelve (12) months from the date of introduction. If Client hires or engages the candidate directly or indirectly during that period, the placement shall be considered sourced by Sales.io and subject to this Agreement.

6. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

  • Participate actively in advisory and coaching sessions
  • Implement agreed systems and processes
  • Provide timely feedback on candidates
  • Provide adequate lead flow to support the placed sales team
  • Provide reasonable onboarding support to hired sales representatives
  • Provide relevant access to CRM, materials, or call recordings when required

Sales.io is not responsible for sales representative performance if recommended systems or processes are not implemented by Client.

7. Independent Contractor Status

Sales representatives introduced or placed by Sales.io are independent candidates and will be contracted or employed directly by Client. Sales.io is not the employer of any hired sales representative and assumes no liability for employment-related matters.

8. Payment Method

All payments must be made in U.S. Dollars (USD) via wire transfer, Stripe, or another mutually agreed electronic payment method. Services will commence after initial payment has been received unless otherwise agreed in writing.

9. Limitation of Liability

Sales.io does not guarantee revenue outcomes, hiring results, or business performance.

Sales.io shall not be liable for:

  • Business losses
  • Lost revenue
  • Indirect damages
  • Consequential damages

Total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount paid by Client.

10. Term

This Agreement becomes effective on the Effective Date. The advisory engagement will run for an initial period of twelve (12) weeks unless extended by mutual written agreement. Replacement guarantee obligations remain active for twelve (12) months from the hired representative's start date.

11. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to maintain confidentiality of proprietary or non-public information shared during this engagement. Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement.

12. Dispute Resolution

The Parties agree to first attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved, disputes shall proceed to binding arbitration in the State of Florida under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

13. General Provisions
  • Governing Law: State of Florida
  • Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties.
  • Amendments: Any amendments must be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
  • Severability: If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in effect.
  • Assignment: Neither Party may assign this Agreement without written consent.
  • Counterparts: This Agreement may be executed electronically and in counterparts.
