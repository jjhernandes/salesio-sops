Section 2

Scope of Services

During the Term of this Agreement, Sales.io agrees to provide the following services to Client (collectively, the "Services"):

1
Sales Rep Sourcing & Placement
Sales.io will source, vet, and place qualified sales representatives on the FitMom Coaching team for the duration of the initial 3-month engagement. This includes:
  • Reviewing candidate profiles from Sales.io's proprietary network
  • Conducting initial screening and vetting on behalf of Client
  • Presenting qualified candidates for Client approval
  • Facilitating the onboarding of placed representatives
  • Replacing underperforming reps within the engagement period at Sales.io's discretion
2
SOP Development
Sales.io will build out a complete set of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) tailored to FitMom Coaching's sales process and offer. This includes:
  • Discovery and qualification call frameworks
  • Pitch and close process documentation
  • Objection handling guides specific to Client's offer
  • Daily and weekly activity standards for the sales team
  • CRM usage and pipeline management protocols
3
Curriculum & Training Access
All placed sales representatives and designated members of the FitMom Coaching team will receive access to the full Sales.io training curriculum for the duration of the Agreement. This includes:
  • Access to Sales.io's proprietary Skool platform and all training modules
  • Identity, psychology, process, and advanced influence curriculum
  • Call recordings, coaching replays, and training materials
4
Coaching Call Access
Placed representatives and eligible team members will have live access to all weekly group coaching calls hosted inside Sales.io, including:
  • Mock Call Monday
  • Setting Mastery
  • Coffee Chat
  • Sales Psychology
  • Call Review / Game Tape
  • Inner Circle Advanced
  • B2B Strategy (where applicable)
5
1-on-1 Coaching Sessions
Sales.io will conduct regular 1-on-1 coaching sessions with Khalil Bass and designated members of the FitMom Coaching team. Session frequency and format to be agreed upon by the Parties at the start of the engagement. These sessions are focused on:
  • Offer and messaging alignment
  • Team performance review
  • Individual rep development
  • Strategic direction and pipeline growth
6
Team Cadence Development
Sales.io will design and implement a team cadence structure for the FitMom Coaching sales team, including:
  • Daily and weekly rhythm for the sales floor
  • Call review and accountability frameworks
  • Performance tracking and KPI structure
  • Communication norms and feedback loops between reps, manager, and Client
7
Sales Manager Placement (Long-Term)
Upon successful completion of the initial 3-month engagement, Sales.io will source and place a qualified Sales Manager on the FitMom Coaching team on a long-term basis. The Sales Manager's responsibilities will include:
  • Day-to-day oversight of the sales team
  • Rep coaching, call review, and performance management
  • Pipeline management and reporting to Client
  • Maintaining and evolving the SOPs developed during the initial engagement

Note: Sales Manager compensation structure (salary, commission, or revenue share) to be agreed upon separately between Client and Sales Manager at the time of placement. Sales.io's role is sourcing and vetting only.

Excluded from Services: Sales.io does not guarantee specific revenue outcomes, close rates, or rep performance levels. Sales.io's obligation is to provide the services described above to the standard of care consistent with its established practices. Individual rep performance is subject to Client's offer quality, lead flow, and market conditions.

Section 3

Term

3.1 Initial Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial period of three (3) months (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 9.

3.2 Continuation

Following the Initial Term, this Agreement may be extended by mutual written agreement of the Parties. Services related to the placed Sales Manager (Section 2.7) shall continue on a month-to-month basis following the Initial Term unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.3 Milestone Review

The Parties agree to conduct a performance and scope review at the end of Month 1 and Month 2 to assess progress, adjust cadence as needed, and prepare for the Sales Manager placement process.

Section 5

Client Obligations

Client agrees to the following in order to enable Sales.io to deliver the Services effectively:

5.1 Cooperation

Client shall provide timely responses, information, and access reasonably required by Sales.io to perform the Services, including offer details, pricing, target market information, and existing sales materials.

5.2 Lead Flow

Client is responsible for providing adequate lead flow to support the placed sales team. Sales.io's rep sourcing and placement services do not include lead generation unless separately agreed in writing.

5.3 Rep Integration

Client agrees to onboard placed representatives into their internal systems (CRM, communication channels, payment platform) within a reasonable timeframe and to provide any necessary access required for the reps to perform their roles.

5.4 Availability

Khalil Bass or a designated decision-maker at FitMom Coaching shall be reasonably available for scheduled 1-on-1 calls, reviews, and onboarding sessions as coordinated by Sales.io.

5.5 Treatment of Sales Reps

Client agrees to treat all placed sales representatives with professionalism and in accordance with applicable employment and contractor laws. Any disputes with placed reps are the responsibility of Client to manage and resolve. Sales.io will assist with replacement sourcing per Section 2.1 but is not an employer of placed representatives.

Section 7

Confidentiality

7.2 Survival

7.3 Non-Disparagement

Section 9

Termination

9.1 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) business days of receiving written notice of the breach.

9.2 Termination Without Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement without cause upon thirty (30) days' written notice. In the event Client terminates without cause, all fees paid for the current service month are non-refundable, and any outstanding balance for the notice period is immediately due.

9.3 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, Client's access to Sales.io's curriculum, training platform, and coaching calls will be revoked. All SOPs and custom deliverables completed and delivered prior to termination are retained by Client per Section 6.2. Placed sales representatives may continue in their roles with Client independent of this Agreement, subject to any separate arrangements between Client and those representatives.
