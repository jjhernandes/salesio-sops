Mindset & Self-Sabotage Patterns

This section is the most important part of the intake. Self-awareness here is what separates clients who transform from clients who quit. There are no wrong answers.

  • All-or-nothing thinking ("if I miss one workout, the week is ruined")
  • Perfectionism (waiting until conditions are "perfect" to start or continue)
  • Emotional eating (food as reward, comfort, or stress response)
  • Self-prioritization struggles (always putting others first)
  • Imposter syndrome (feeling like you don't "deserve" to succeed yet)
  • Motivation dependency (only consistent when feeling motivated)
  • Comparison to others (measuring your progress against other people's highlight reels)
  • Fear of failure (avoiding commitment to avoid the risk of not succeeding)
  • Binge-restrict cycle (swinging between extremes)
