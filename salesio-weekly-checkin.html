Coach SOP — Student Experience

Weekly 1-on-1 Check-In

Every student gets a weekly 1-on-1. This is the standard structure so every coach runs it the same way — consistent enough to spot problems early, flexible enough to actually coach the person in front of you.

FrequencyWeekly, all 12 weeks
Length20-30 min
OwnerAssigned coach
Logged InClient Hub
1

Purpose & Cadence

Why this exists
Group calls teach the framework. The 1-on-1 is where you find out if it's actually landing for this specific person — what's blocking them, what they're avoiding, and whether they're on pace for the guarantees they signed up for.
📅
CadenceOne check-in per student, every week, for all 12 weeks. Same day/time each week where possible — consistency is part of the coaching.
2

Pre-Call Prep

3

Call Structure

SegmentTimeWhat happens
Wins3-5 minWhat went well this week — calls, mindset, discipline. Make them say it out loud.
Metrics review5-8 minCall volume, close rate trend vs. baseline, any tracked numbers.
Obstacle5-8 minThe one thing actually holding them back this week. Not five things — one.
Coaching5-8 minDirect instruction tied to that obstacle, referencing the framework they're on.
This week's focus2-3 minOne clear, specific commitment for the coming week.
4

Question Bank

  • Walk me through your best call this week. What made it work?
  • Where in the call architecture are you losing people — discovery, pitch, or objections?
  • What's your close rate this week vs. your baseline?
  • Who were you being on your calls this week — is that who you actually want to be?
  • Where did you feel the most certain? The least?
  • What story are you telling yourself about why this isn't working yet?
  • What did you commit to last week that you didn't follow through on?
  • What's the actual obstacle — skill, mindset, or volume?
  • What do you need from me, from the community, or from the job platform to move faster?
5

Escalation Triggers

If any of these show up, update the student's status in Client Hub immediately — don't wait for next week's check-in.

Flag as Renewal Due
Student has hit Week 10+ and hasn't been sent the renewal doc yet. Trigger the Renewal SOP.
Flag as At Risk
Two or more missed group calls in a row, no check-in commitment follow-through for two consecutive weeks, or the student says anything indicating they're considering quitting. Loop in Cole the same day.
