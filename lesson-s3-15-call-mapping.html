Call Mapping Exercise

Pull a real call from this week. Map it against the 12 steps. Be honest. This is the fastest way to find exactly where your calls break down.

How to Use This Worksheet

One call at a time. Pull a Zoom recording from this week — ideally one you didn't close. Score each step honestly. A 1 or 2 isn't failure, it's a diagnostic. The rep who lies on this worksheet gets nothing from it.

Don't do this from memory. Actually play the recording. Pause it at each step. You'll be wrong about what you think happened on your calls.

1 — Skipped or weak
2 — Attempted, not complete
3 — Executed well
