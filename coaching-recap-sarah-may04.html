The Biggest Unlock — Webinar Closes Are Different

This is the single most important thing from this session. Sarah — and most closers on webinar offers — are treating webinar closes like book-a-call closes. They are not the same. At all.

Book-a-Call Close
  • Prospect opted in intentionally
  • Higher intent coming in
  • Longer qualification cycle
  • Buying decision happens over time
Webinar Close
  • Prospect was educated first by the webinar
  • Different emotional state on the call
  • Totally different flow and frame
  • Different process from open to close

"I know a guy who works 2 to 3 days a week and does about $30K a month with webinar closes. The process in which he takes is very, very different."

JJ

You don't need a huge team. You don't need more reps. You need more predictable processes that are built specifically for a webinar-based prospect. That's what's missing, and it's the biggest lever available right now.

The 15-Minute Audit Log

From Hormozi. Every 15 minutes, you write down exactly what you did and the activity. It's not about time management — it's about seeing reality. Most people discover they're losing 3-4 hours a day to things they can't account for.

→

If you were on a 45-minute call, you'd log: on call / on call / on call in three 15-minute blocks. That's it. Simple. But it exposes everything. JJ will send the Hormozi version — it's cleaner than what he showed today.

The point isn't perfection — it's data. You can't improve what you can't see. Same principle as tracking dials and connects. Apply it to your time.
