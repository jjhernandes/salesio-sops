Internal SOP — Guarantees

Close Rate Guarantee Verification

How the guaranteed 10%+ close rate increase gets measured, tracked, and verified for every student — from baseline to final number. No guessing, no "it feels higher."

Guarantee10%+ close rate increase
BaselineCaptured Week 1
CheckpointsWeek 6 · Week 12
1

Guarantee Terms

📈
What we promiseA minimum 10 percentage-point increase in close rate, measured against the student's own documented baseline — not against a company average or another student.
Percentage points, not percent of percent
A student starting at 15% must reach 25%+ to satisfy the guarantee — not 16.5%. This distinction matters and should be stated plainly to every student at baseline capture.
2

Baseline Capture

Done once, in Week 1, with the assigned coach. No baseline captured means no guarantee to verify against — this step is non-negotiable.

⚠️
Complete beginners30-40% of students have zero sales experience. For these students, baseline is 0% and the guarantee becomes "reach 10%+ close rate by Week 12" — flag this distinction in Client Hub so it isn't misread later.

4

Verification Checkpoints

Week 6 — Midpoint Check Trend Check
Not a pass/fail moment. Confirm the student's close rate is trending upward and flag anyone flat or declining for extra coaching focus in Phase 2.
Week 12 — Final Verification Guarantee Check
Final close rate logged against baseline. This is the number that determines whether the guarantee is satisfied.
5

If Not On Track

1
Flag at Week 6
Any student flat or declining vs. baseline gets flagged in Client Hub and reviewed by Cole.
Owner: Assigned coach
2
Targeted intervention
Extra call reviews, additional mock calls, and a specific weekly focus area for the remainder of the program.
Owner: Cole + assigned coach
3
Week 12 shortfall
If the 10%+ increase still isn't met at Week 12, this becomes a guarantee claim — hand off to the Guarantee Claim SOP.
Owner: Cole → JJ
