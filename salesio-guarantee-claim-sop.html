ROI Guarantee Claim SOP

The process for handling a money-back guarantee claim end to end. We've had zero disputes and one refund in 549 payments — this SOP exists so the next one, whenever it comes, gets handled fast and fair with zero ambiguity.

GuaranteeInvestment back in 90 days
OwnerCole → JJ (final sign-off)
SLA5 business days to decision
1

Guarantee Terms

📌
What we promiseMake your $7,500 investment back within 90 days of your start date, or receive a full refund. This is stated in the Enrollment Agreement, Section 3.
Window starts
Day 1 of the cohort — the date recorded on the signed Enrollment Agreement, not the payment date if they differ.
Window closes
Day 90. Claims must be submitted by Day 95 (a 5-day grace period for students who are close but need a few extra days to document results).
2

Eligibility

A claim is only valid if the student can demonstrate applied effort. Attendance alone doesn't qualify — the guarantee is tied to doing the work.

⚠️
If eligibility isn't metDon't deny outright. Loop in Cole — there may be a legitimate reason (medical, family emergency) that warrants an exception, same as our one prior refund.
3

Claim Process

1
Student submits claim
In writing, via Slack or email, before Day 95. Must state the investment hasn't been made back and reference the guarantee.
Owner: Student
2
Pull eligibility record
Attendance, 1-on-1 history, call submissions, job platform activity — from Client Hub and Skool.
Owner: Cole
3
Coach review call
A direct conversation with the student's coach — what was applied, what wasn't, and why the investment wasn't made back.
Owner: Assigned coach + Cole
4
Recommendation to JJ
Cole brings a recommendation — approve, deny, or extend coaching by 2-4 weeks in lieu of refund — with the eligibility record attached.
Owner: Cole → JJ
5
Final decision
JJ signs off. Decision communicated to the student within 5 business days of the original claim.
Owner: JJ
