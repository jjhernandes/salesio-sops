JJ
Don't Rush It

Ryan's instinct to close first and earn the right to manage later is the correct one. Don't let ambition pull you into a role that pays less and takes more out of you before you're ready for it.

Your Biggest Competition Is You

JJ was clear: the fundamentals are there. Ryan's sales instincts are solid. The thing most likely to slow him down is not the offer, not the show rate, not the market — it's the story he tells himself about whether he's ready.

"Your biggest discrepancy is gonna be yourself every time. I have 18 and 19 year olds in this community doing 40 and 50K a month. That's not talent — that's them not getting in their own way."

The belief bottleneck looks like this: "I'm not sure I have the skill set to land a role like this." When JJ pushes — why not? — the answer falls apart. The concern isn't real. It's fear dressed as humility. Name it and move through it.
