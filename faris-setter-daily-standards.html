How to Log a Note

Every note needs three things
1. What happened on the conversation
2. What the next step is
3. Who owns the next step and when
Bad Note
"Spoke to him. Will follow up."
Good Note
"Connected. He's a nurse looking to transition into sales. Said he's tried cold outreach before but never got traction. Interested in the program but wants to talk to his wife first. Following up Thursday afternoon his time. — Faris"
✍️
Always sign your notes.End every note with "— Faris" so JJ knows at a glance who logged it without having to dig.
4

What Good Looks Like

End of every day, your Close should show:
✓ Every lead you contacted today has a note
✓ Every booked call is in "Call Booked" with date confirmed
✓ Every new lead has a real name and a $7,500 opportunity
✓ No lead untouched for 7+ days without a follow-up task
✓ Zero duplicates created today
JJ's job in Close is to read, not to write.
If JJ is logging leads, updating stages, or adding notes — that's on you. The CRM is your responsibility. Keep it clean and JJ can run the business from it. Let it get dirty and it's useless to everyone.
