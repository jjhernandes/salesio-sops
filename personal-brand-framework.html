Personal Brand — JJ Hernandez

Personal Brand Framework

The voice, pillars, and positioning behind everything JJ posts. Use this before writing any content — LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, all of it. If it doesn't sound like this, it's not on brand.

The Line
"Everyone else teaches you what to say. We teach you how to think."
1

Positioning

The mission
Change the world of sales — how it's taught, how it's practiced, and how it's perceived. Not "get better at closing." Change what people think a salesperson even is.
The anti-guru stance
JJ positions directly against the scripted, tactic-first gurus (Jeremy Miner, Cole Gordon, Andy Elliott). Their content teaches lines to say. JJ's content teaches the psychology underneath — identity, communication, human behavior — so the person never sounds like they're running a script.
2

Brand Voice

Four qualities every piece of content should have.
Raw Authenticity
Conversational, not corporate. Sounds like JJ actually talking, not a brand voice guideline being followed.
Earned Authority
Certain, backed by real results and real names. Not hype — receipts. $20M+ closed, 2,100+ reps coached, 100% one-on-one success rate.
Tough Love
Direct care, not hand-holding. "We find out where you suck and make you good" — said with love, not cruelty.
Anti-Guru
Positioned against the scripted, hype-driven sales influencer space. No fake urgency, no manufactured scarcity, no guru theater.

4

Content Pillars

What JJ's content is actually about, split four ways.
Psychology & Identity
The "how to think" content — cognitive dissonance, the Four Wounds, frame control, communication theory. This is the core differentiator, post it most.
Real Results
Track record content — student wins, income jumps, job placements, call breakdowns. Receipts, not claims.
Anti-Guru Takes
Direct commentary on why scripted sales training fails, without naming names unnecessarily. Contrarian, backed by reasoning.
Behind the Coaching
Real coaching moments — call reviews, mock calls, 1-on-1 breakthroughs. Shows the work, not just the outcome.
5

Platforms

Each platform plays a different role — don't post the same thing everywhere the same way.
YouTube
Primary Inbound Driver
Long-form, coaching recordings, VSL, testimonials. This is the biggest lead source — treat it as the flagship channel, not an afterthought.
LinkedIn
Authority Building
Thought leadership, psychology breakdowns, anti-guru takes. This is where the "earned authority" pillar does the most work.
Instagram
Reach & DM Lead Gen
Reels and Stories for reach, DMs for direct community and lead conversations. More casual, more raw, faster-moving.
6

Who This Is For

Content should speak to this person specifically — not "sales professionals" in general.
The ideal viewer
A sales rep already making $5K–$15K/month in commissions, who knows they're capable of more, has tried tactical approaches (scripts, NEPQ, etc.) without a real breakthrough, and is ready to go to $20K–$40K+ months.
Avoid speaking to
Generic "entrepreneur" audience People who just want scripts Get-rich-quick framing
