Job Placement Guarantee

Every student who applies themselves lands a role. Every student has landed a role within their first month so far — this SOP is how we keep that streak alive as we scale past $100K/month.

GuaranteePlacement on $100K-$1M+/mo offer
Track record100% within first month
Platform60-80+ vetted roles, refreshed daily
1

Guarantee Terms

💼
What we promisePlacement on a vetted offer doing $100K-$1M+/month in revenue, contingent on active use of the job platform and application process throughout the program.
This is a guarantee of access and effort, not a passive outcome
We guarantee a real pipeline of vetted, high-revenue offers and hands-on application support. The student still has to show up to interviews and apply the pitch skills they're learning.

If Not Placed

1
30 days post-completion, no placement
Flag in Client Hub automatically. Jalen reviews application volume and interview conversion for gaps.
Owner: Jalen
2
Diagnose the gap
Not enough applications, weak Loom pitches, or interview performance — identify which, then fix that specific thing.
Owner: Jalen + assigned coach
3
Extended support window
Continued job platform access and weekly check-ins until placement, at no additional cost, as long as the student stays active in the process.
Owner: Jalen
4
Effort breakdown
If the student stops applying or stops showing up to interviews, the guarantee obligation shifts — this becomes a Guarantee Claim SOP conversation with Cole and JJ.
Owner: Cole → JJ
