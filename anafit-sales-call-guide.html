Ana Fit VIP · Sales Call Framework

Your Sales Call, Start to Finish

The shape of this call stays the same every time. What changes is who's in front of you — plug in what they actually tell you as you go.

~30–40 minutes
Works for any prospect
Listen more than you talk
1
Open Warm
~3 min
Set the tone before anything else happens.
Say
"Hey [Name]! So glad we're finally talking."
"Before we get into anything — how's your day going?"
"I want this to just be a real conversation, not a pitch. Sound good?"
2
Understand Where They Are Now
~7 min
Get the full picture before you say a word about the program.
Say
"Tell me where you're at right now with your fitness and health."
"What have you tried before?"
"What's working, what's not?"
💗
Listen more than you talk here. The whole rest of the call gets built from what they say now.
3
Find the Real Problem
~5 min
The first answer is rarely the real one — go one layer deeper.
Say
"What do you think's actually gotten in the way?"
"How long has this been going on?"
"What's it costing you — energy, confidence, time with your family?"
4
Paint the Future
~5 min
Let them describe the win in their own words — don't describe it for them.
Say
"If this actually worked, what would be different?"
"How would you feel walking into a room?"
"What would you be able to do that you can't right now?"
5
Bridge to the Program
~3 min
Summarize what you heard, then ask permission before you pitch anything.
Say
"Based on everything you just told me, here's what I'm hearing..."
[Reflect their goal and their problem back in one sentence]
"Can I walk you through exactly how I'd help you get there?"
6
Present the Program
~7 min
Tie every piece back to something they actually said — never list features in a vacuum.
Say
"This is a 12-week program, built completely around you — not a template."
"You get a custom training and nutrition plan, biweekly 1-on-1 calls with me, weekly check-ins I personally read, and direct access to me the whole way through."
"We start with a full movement assessment so we know exactly where your body's at before anything else."
🌸
Reference: full program breakdown lives in the offer doc — pull specific pieces that match what this person told you in steps 2–4, don't recite the whole list.
7
Confirm It's a Fit
~2 min
Get them to say yes before you ever say a number.
Say
"Does this feel like the right fit for what you're trying to solve?"
"What about it stands out to you most?"
8
Share the Investment
~3 min
Say it plainly. Don't shrink it, don't over-explain it, don't apologize for it.
Say
"The program is [$X]."
"Is that something you can make work?"
9
Handle Objections
~5 min
These aren't rejections — they're the real conversation starting.
"It's a lot of money."
"Is it that it's not possible, or that you're not fully sure yet if it's worth it?"
"I don't have the time."
"What would need to be true for this to actually fit your schedule?"
"I've tried programs before and they didn't work."
"What was different about those? This one's built around your body and your life, not a template — that's usually the gap."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course — what specifically do you want to think through?"
⚠️
Don't argue. Get curious, not defensive. The objection stage should feel calm, not like a debate you're trying to win.
10
Close
~3 min
Ask directly. Then stop talking.
Say
"Based on everything we talked about — are you ready to start?"
[Wait. Let them answer. Don't fill the silence.]
✅
If yes: get payment and their first session on the calendar before the call ends — don't leave with "I'll send the link."
🌸
If no, or not now: "Totally fine — the door's open whenever it's right." No pressure, no guilt. Follow up warmly in a week.
