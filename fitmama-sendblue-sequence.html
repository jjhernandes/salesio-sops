The Handoff Rule

Two people are legitimately texting from this number now, in sequence. The rule isn't "always say who you are immediately" — it's "never let anyone be confused about who they're talking to for longer than one message."

Do

Open as Jaz. Same energy as the incomplete-application flow — this is the account they were just DMing, so the thread should read as unbroken.

Don't

Don't let Jaz linger. Once the booking's confirmed, her job is a warm handoff, not ongoing conversation — dragging it out is what actually breaks trust, not the handoff itself.

Do

Have the closer name-check Jaz the moment they take over ("jaz looped me in"). That one line is what makes the switch feel like a introduction, not a bait-and-switch.

Don't

No pitching, no program details, no pricing from either persona in this sequence. Its only jobs: warm handoff, gather context, get them prepped, get them to show up.
