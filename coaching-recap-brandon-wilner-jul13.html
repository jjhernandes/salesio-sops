Main Coaching Moment

The Anti-Fragility Framework

Concept — Ali (NASA / SpaceX-adjacent mentor)
Anticipate failure so it can't break you
Desired Outcome
The real goal — ambitious but specific. Not a vague income number, a clear state to be in.
Failure + Vision
Not zero. What does failure actually look like based on your history? When's the last time you truly made zero? Failure envisioned should be near your current average floor — realistic, not catastrophic.
Resources
What's missing right now that would close the gap between where you are and the desired outcome?
Initiation
Once resources are in place, move with speed. Limited time to act before the window closes.
Breaking Point
You'll hit a moment where it's clear you're not going to hit the target this cycle. Confirm failure early — don't grind into a wall. Iterate instead.
Iterate
Go back. Same desired outcome, updated failure vision, refined resources. The cycle repeats — but faster and smarter each time.
