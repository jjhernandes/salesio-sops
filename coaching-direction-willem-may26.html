We call these the Four Wounds.

You can't fully destroy a wall if you don't know why it was built. The work isn't just "push through the anxiety." The work is tracing back to where that belief was formed, understanding it wasn't a fact — it was a decision — and consciously replacing it. That's the level underneath what you wrote, and that's where the real work lives.
